OpenSpec artifacts are authoritative project records.

Use complete and unambiguous sentences.
Keep requirements concise by removing repetition, not necessary meaning.
Every requirement must be testable.
Separate mandatory behavior from implementation suggestions.
Do not use telegraphic or caveman-style fragments for requirements,
security controls, migration steps, rollback plans, or acceptance criteria.
